🐦 Chirp Code of Conduct

Welcome to Chirp! Before joining, please read and agree to our community rules.

✅ You agree to:

  • Be respectful to all members of the Chirp community
  • Post only content that is appropriate for all ages
  • Protect the privacy of yourself and others
  • Report harmful content rather than engaging with it
  • Take responsibility for everything you post

🚫 You agree NOT to:

  • Use profanity or offensive language — this is strictly enforced and filtered automatically
  • Bully, harass, or threaten any other user
  • Share personal information of others without consent
  • Post spam, misleading content, or impersonate others
  • Share explicit, violent, or illegal content of any kind
  • Create multiple accounts to evade bans or rules

🔒 Your Security:

  • Your account is secured — never share your password
  • Use a strong, unique password for your Chirp account
  • Log out on shared or public devices
  • Report any suspected unauthorized access immediately

Violations may result in immediate account suspension. Chirp reserves the right to remove any content or account that violates these guidelines.
