🐦 Chirp Code of Conduct

Welcome to Chirp! Please read and agree to our community rules before joining.

✅ You agree to:

  • Be respectful to all members of the Chirp community
  • Post only content appropriate for all ages
  • Protect the privacy of yourself and others
  • Take responsibility for everything you post

🚫 You agree NOT to:

  • Use profanity or offensive language — automatically filtered
  • Bully, harass, or threaten any other user
  • Share personal information of others without consent
  • Post spam, misleading content, or impersonate others
  • Share explicit, violent, or illegal content

🔒 Security:

  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Log out on shared or public devices
  • Report any unauthorized access immediately

Violations may result in immediate account suspension.
